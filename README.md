Participated in an intriguing and exciting event - 🚀 Agent IQ X salesforce. Which was a 2-day workshop followed by a 24hr breathtaking Hackathon.

We  learned quite a bit about agents and how agents are built in #agentforce.

>What did we Build 💡 ⁉️ 
We Team #Shanmukha

Built a "Lost card concierge" Agent whose job is to assist client who are dealing with a lost credit/debit card situation. The "Lost Card Concierge" Agent 
understands the clients situation and assists them with precise and needed services like freezing and unfreezing cards and also providing replacement services. Sounds good right!!!

>what about security and verification ⁉️

Good question! Well before providing clients with any kind of service , our agents asks for proper credentials like Registered Name and last four digits of your card. If the authenticity of the details are verified from the database and then the client is provided access to freeze and unfreeze the specific card.

>Why this out of Manyy ⁉️ 

Again an intriguing question! This idea may seem generic but the significance of such an agent is high, the reason for that being high Credit/Debit card Frauds Rates(its nearly 1457cr annually) , that's a huge number yet there is no significant solution for the problem till date. That's where "Lost Card concierge " Agent comes into play ,even a slightest doubt or suspicion of fraud, verify your credentials and with a single command Booom!!! your card is frozen. Bad day for the fraudster.

>What could we have done more or different ‼️ 

With the time constraints we were given , we worked on every solution we could think off and i am really happy with that, " you know there is a 'but' coming !" but seeing our project from a different angle ,i think these are the things i would do differently


1> Increase the security verification by adding mobile/email verification.

2>Put some more variation in the customer response dataset.

3>Provide address verification after a replacement request and maybe a new address form as well.
I think these are the things that could have increased the appeal of our agent even more.
